Chicken Tikka

Ingredients

  1. 800 gm boneless chicken
  2. 1 tsp jeera powder
  3. 1 tsp laal mirch
  4. 1 tsp dhaniya powder
  5. 1 tsp garam masala
  6. 1 tsp tandoori chicken masala
  7. 1 tsp salt
  8. 1/2 lemon juice
  9. 1 tsp adrak lehsun paste
  10. 4 tsp gada(thick) dahi
  11. 1 tsp orange food color
  12. 1 tsp refined oil

Utensils

  1. Bamboo/Steel sticks
  2. Bowl
  3. Water
  4. OTG/Tandoor
